# Advertiser Integrity

The product separates four levels of analysis:

- content-level creative evidence;
- campaign-level repetition when campaign links exist;
- advertiser-level history when authorized identifiers and history exist;
- network-level linkage only when authorized relationship evidence exists.

The public snapshot has no authorized Meta creative records, so the real-profile section is empty. It does not invent advertisers, velocity, appeals, domains, or linked entities.

Five clearly named `Benchmark Advertiser` profiles aggregate the ten curated behavior scenarios. These are hypothetical evaluation entities, never real companies and never included in public-record KPIs.

Recommended actions are analyst investigation prompts. They are not identity judgments, account enforcement, or claims of coordinated behavior.
