## Summary
Meta restricts ads for financial products and services such as loans, credit, and insurance, with transparency requirements and, in some markets, verification or authorization; deceptive or predatory financial offers are prohibited. Treat no-credit-check or guaranteed-approval lending and unlicensed offers as high-risk.
