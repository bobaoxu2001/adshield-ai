## Summary
Google requires financial-services ads to disclose complete and accurate terms (rates, fees, and risks) and to comply with local licensing and regulation. It restricts deceptive or predatory offerings and certain unregulated or high-risk financial products. Treat guaranteed returns, no-credit-check lending, and unlicensed or anonymous offers as high-risk.
