## Summary
Meta restricts ads for health, dietary, and weight-loss products, prohibiting claims that promote negative self-perception or unrealistic outcomes and requiring truthful representation. Treat rapid-weight-loss, miracle-cure, and before/after outcome claims as high-salience evidence.
