## Summary
Financial-services advertisers generally need appropriate licensing, legally required disclosures, and adult targeting. Get-rich-quick, pyramid, too-good-to-be-true, payday, credit-repair, and other high-risk offers may be prohibited or market-restricted; local rules remain decisive.
