## Summary
Google Ads prohibits ads and destinations that deceive users by omitting relevant information or providing misleading information, including unclear billing or subscription terms, unavailable offers, unrealistic "get rich quick" guarantees, and clickbait or manipulated creatives. Treat absolute outcome claims, hidden costs, and sensational bait as high-salience review evidence.
