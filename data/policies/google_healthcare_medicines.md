## Summary
Google restricts promotion of prescription drugs, unapproved pharmaceuticals, and misleading health claims; some healthcare advertising requires certification and is limited by location. Treat cure-all, miracle, and unverified efficacy or rapid-outcome claims as high-salience evidence.
